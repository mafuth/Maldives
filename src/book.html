Avilable Resorts

ADAARAN CLUB RANNALHI

Adaaran Club Rannalhi sits exclusively at the tip of the South Malé atoll within the exotic collection of islands known as the Maldives.

AMARI HAVODDA MALDIVES

Designed within a theme of “natural simplicity”, Amari Havodda Maldives’ public areas and villas depict subtle elements from local island culture with carefully selected artwork.

AMAYA KUDA RAH MALDIVES

Situated in the heart of South Ari Atoll, Amaya Kuda Rah Maldives is just a 25-minute seaplane or domestic flight ride followed by a 15-minute transfer via speedboats or traditional dhoni.

ANGAGA ISLAND RESORT & SPA

Angaga Island Resort and Spa is one of the most beautiful resorts in the Maldives. Its unique natural beauty is complemented by the fine white sand of its beach and the crystal-clear lagoon.
